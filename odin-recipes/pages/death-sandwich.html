Death Sandwich

A sandwich that kills if not eaten with proper clothing

Background Origin: Regular Show

A sandwich created by an ancient dojo sect thousands of years ago, and was a closely guarded secret. Eventually, a sensai took the recipe and made it into a commercial product. The sandwich will kill the consumer if not eaten correctly. One must wear cut-off jean shorts and a mullet in order to eat it safely.

If eaten incorrectly, the consumer will suffer a deadly illness. Sufferers will undergo six symptoms over the course of six hours. Victim's face will start to turn green and they start choking and gagging; Their face will start to feel like it's on fire; Their intestines melt away and leak out of their body. The victim will then shriek in pain, but no one will hear them since they lose their voice. They will then regain their voice, only to cry out for mercy in ancient, forgotten dialects. It will obviously conclude with death.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Spaghetti Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup BBQ Sauce
  • Split Top Sub Rolls
  • Package of Sliced Sandwich Pepperoni
  • Package of Small Sliced Canadian Bacon
  • Meatballs
  • 1 lb. Ground Beef
  • 1 lb. Ground Turkey
  • 4 oz. Prosciutto, chopped
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/4 Cup Shredded Parmesan
  • 2 Tbsp. Plain Bread Crumbs
  • 1 Tbsp. Fresh Italian Parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp. Salt
  • Pinch of Ground Black Pepper

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350ÂºF. Spray a large baking dish with non-stick spray.
  2. In a large bowl, squish together the meatball ingredients until well combined. Roll into balls and place them in the baking dish in a single layer.
  3. Cover with foil & bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through.
  4. Transfer meatballs to a container and discard the juices from the baking tray.
  5. In a medium bowl, stir together spaghetti sauce with BBQ sauce, and spread mixture on the cut open sub roll.
  6. Fold several slices of sandwich pepperoni and Canadian bacon. Lay them alternately across the bottom half of the sub roll.
  7. Top with a row of meatballs. If desired, add shredded parmesan, chopped parsley, and close the top of sub roll.
  8. Congrats, you created the Death Sandwich. Remember to wear cut-off jeans and a mullet while eating it.
